## STRINGED & FRETTED INSTRUMENTS
- Eligible for full refund within 45 days of purchase if unused and in original condition with all original packaging and accessories
- Open-box or display models: store credit only, no cash refund
- Used/vintage instruments: all sales final, no returns

## AMPLIFIERS & ELECTRONICS
- Eligible for full refund within 30 days, unopened only
- Opened amplifiers: exchange or store credit within 15 days
- No returns on amplifiers with visible wear or missing components

## SOFTWARE & DIGITAL DOWNLOADS
- All sales final, no exceptions
- No refunds on activated or registered software licenses

## ACCESSORIES (strings, picks, cables, straps)
- Refund within 15 days, unopened packaging only
- Opened accessories: no refund, no exchange

## DRUMS & PERCUSSION
- Eligible for full refund within 30 days, unplayed and in original packaging
- Cymbals: no returns once opened

## PRO AUDIO & RECORDING EQUIPMENT
- Refund within 30 days, unopened only
- Opened pro audio: store credit only within 14 days

## KEYBOARDS & MIDI CONTROLLERS
- Eligible for full refund within 30 days of purchase if unopened and in original packaging with all included accessories
- Floor models and open-box purchases: all sales final, no refunds or exchanges
- Opened keyboards or controllers: store credit only within 14 days, no cash refund
- MIDI controllers with registered software bundles: software component is non-refundable once activated, hardware only eligible for store credit within 14 days

## SHEET MUSIC & BOOKS
- All sales final once packaging is opened or binding is creased
- Unopened sheet music and books eligible for full refund within 15 days of purchase with original receipt
- Digital sheet music downloads: all sales final, no exceptions
- Damaged or defective items eligible for exchange only, no cash refund

## GENERAL CONDITIONS
- Original receipt required for all returns
- Refunds processed to original payment method within 5-7 business days
- Items showing signs of use, modification, or damage are ineligible
- Holiday purchases eligible for extended return window through January 31
- Manager approval required for refunds over $500
